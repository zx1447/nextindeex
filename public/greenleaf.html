Where AI Meets Charity

In 2024, GreenLeaf Foundation launched the AI Image Generator project to provide free visual creation tools for grassroots NGOs without design resources. From mountain school event notices to community eco-campaigns, professional posters can now be generated in one click.

The generator is also open to the public. Every creation becomes a contribution to charity - quality works are curated into a public asset library, free for any NGO to use.

We believe technology should be an amplifier for kindness. Make creation simpler. Make charity more visible.
